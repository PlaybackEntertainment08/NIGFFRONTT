Imm Past President

Dr Sadhna Gupta

It is with great honor and humility that I take on the responsibility of leading the New Incredible Gynaecology Forum (NIGF). This forum is not just an association, but a vision—built to bring together the Ob-Gyn fraternity of North India with a shared commitment to women’s health.

At NIGF, we believe:
“To care for a woman’s health is to safeguard the health of generations.” Our mission is to provide a platform that fosters collaboration, encourages innovation, and drives impactful initiatives in clinical practice, research, and education. Leadership, for me, is not about position—it is about creating opportunities for growth, inclusivity, and meaningful change.
