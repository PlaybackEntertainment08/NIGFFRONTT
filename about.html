Our Motto

“Academic Excellence – Unity – Safety”

Academic Excellence

  • Training of Trainers (TOT) programs to develop leadership among Ob- Gyn specialists.
  • Continuing E-Education, workshops, and journal clubs.
  • Opportunities for research, paper presentations, and case discussions.

Unity

  • Regular meetings, conferences, and cross-state collaborations to foster bonding and togetherness.

Medicolegal Safety

  • Educating members about essential medicolegal laws, rights, and responsibilities to safeguard their practice in today’s challenging world.

Social Commitments

At NIGF, we believe in serving society beyond the clinic. Our flagship community programs include:

Anemia Mukt Bharat – Fighting anemia in women across North India.

NIGF Women Cancer Prevention Awareness Mission

  • - Cervical Cancer awareness & screening
  • - Breast Cancer – Early detection saves lives

Preventing The Preventable Causes of Maternal Mortality Phase 1 PPH – Reducing maternal mortality through education on postpartum hemorrhage prevention.

Support for Sexual Assault Survivors – Training doctors to examine and assist victims with compassion and legal awareness.

Addressing Birth Defects and Fetal Optimization – Spreading awareness on early detection and preventive care.

Mode of Working

Central TOT → Trains State Trainers

State Trainers → Train City Trainers

City Trainers → Train local Ob-Gyn specialists

This structured model ensures consistent training, knowledge sharing, and outreach at every level.
