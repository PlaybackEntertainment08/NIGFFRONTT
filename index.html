Who We Are

NIGF - New Incredible Gynaecology Forum

The New Incredible Gynaecology Forum (NIGF) is a pioneering professional body dedicated to advancing women’s health and uniting the Obstetrics and Gynaecology fraternity of North India. NIGF was founded with a vision to create a collaborative platform where clinicians, researchers, educators, and young professionals can come together to share knowledge, drive innovation, and advocate for women’s health at every stage of life. We believe that:

“To care for a woman’s health is to safeguard the health of generations.”

Dr. Sharda Jain Keynote

It is great pleasure to welcome you all in North India Gynaecologist Forum (NIGF) After many years of uncertainty, NIGF has taken shape & founded on 20/07/2022 with support of 101 stallwarts, representing 8 states & 2 union territories i.e. Kashmir , Jammu , Himachal , Chandigarh , Punjab , Haryana , Delhi , Uttrakhand, Uttarpradesh , Rajsthan Government of India's spent on us 4 crores of public exchanger money to makes a doctor and specialist.

Now our job is to give back with interest by making women and girls of this country healthy. Think tank of NIGF identified five clear, important doable health challenges facing the doctors, women and girls of India where gynaecologist can contribute tremendously to set them right. And these are Anemia Mukt Bharat / Cervical Cancer Mukt Bharat / The State of ART Examination of Rape Survivor / decrease PPH mortality to 50% in the next 4 years / Regain unity and dignity of medical fraternity and save doctors from public violence and litigations.

The key to growth and excellence in performance in these key areas has few fundamentals We talk the same language and do update our self with NIGF training material ; Doing the doable, sharing vision / mission , sharing solutions. Become Ambassador and step out of our comfort zone & do social networking to train our large force of NIGF member and others do job with 100% dedication & security in nation building . I am sure NIGF will become largest vibrant academic platform for knowledge exchange, medico legal awareness , unity of doctors & where every member matters.

President's Keynote

Namaskar and Warm Greetings,

It is with great honor and humility that I take on the responsibility of leading the New Incredible Gynaecology Forum (NIGF). This forum is not just an association, but a vision—built to bring together the Ob-Gyn fraternity of North India with a shared commitment to women’s health.

At NIGF, we believe:
“To care for a woman’s health is to safeguard the health of generations.” Our mission is to provide a platform that fosters collaboration, encourages innovation, and drives impactful initiatives in clinical practice, research, and education. Leadership, for me, is not about position—it is about creating opportunities for growth, inclusivity, and meaningful change.
